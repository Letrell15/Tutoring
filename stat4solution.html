If the two balls are drawn without replacement, then answer 1 through 4.

1. Find P(both balls are black). 

The probability of drawing a black ball is 4 out of 10 and drawing another black ball without replacing 

the first one is 3 out of 9. Multiply these two probabilities and simplify them and the answer will be 2 out of 15.

2. Find P(the first ball is blue and the second ball is red).

The probability of drawing a blue ball is 3 out of 10 and drawing a red ball without replacing the first one 

is 2 out of 9. Multiply these two probabilities and simplify them and the answer will be 1 out of 15.

3. Find P(the first ball is black and the second is not green).

The probability of drawing a black ball is 3 out of 10 and drawing a ball that is not green without replacing the first one 

is 8 out of 9. Multiply these two probabilities and simplify them and the answer will be 16 out of 45.

4. Find P(both balls are odd).

The probability of drawing an odd ball is 6 out of 10 and drawing another ball that is odd without replacing the first one 

is 5 out of 9. Multiply these two probabilities and simplify them and the answer will be 1 out of 3.
